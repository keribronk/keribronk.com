Keri Bronk is a graphic designer and brand consultant based in New York City. Her work focuses primarily on design strategy and visual identity implementation, but also includes publications, printed matter, exhibition design, and websites for cultural, fashion, and entertainment institutions. She has worked on identity projects with Aesop, The Wall Street Journal, Whitney Museum of American Art, Mansur Gavriel, and The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Other clients include Converse, Yale University, and Massachusetts Museum of Contemporary Art.

Her work has been recognized by international organizations such as BRNO Graphic Design Competition and D&AD. Keri holds an MFA from Yale University and a BA in Product Design from Philadelphia University. She is currently an Adjunct Instructor at Parsons School of Design.
